About this site

Ophthalmic AI Paper Hub is a paper-sharing website focused on collecting, summarizing, and organizing important ophthalmic AI papers.

Curated, not exhaustive

The goal is to surface useful papers with high signal, not to dump every loosely related item.

Original links first

Whenever possible, entries point to journal pages, DOI pages, official conference pages, or original preprints.

Fast scanning

Each paper card is designed for fast reading: title, venue, summary, tags, link, and a reserved model-figure slot.

Continuously updated

The structure is ready for scheduled updates so new papers, improved summaries, and model figures can be added over time.

A living ophthalmic AI paper-sharing resource.
